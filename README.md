# Business Operations System (BOS)

Business Operations System (BOS) is a scalable Spring Boot backend platform built to manage business workflows such as order management, kitchen workflow, billing, and payments.

The initial MVP is focused on small hotels, dhabas, and restaurants, with future scalability for:

- Medical Stores
- Small Supermarkets
- Retail Shops
- Other SME businesses

The goal is to build a modular and scalable platform similar in vision to Square, starting with hospitality and expanding across multiple business domains.
